Description

This baked mostaccioli with meatballs is an incredibly easy dish to prepare for a potluck dinner, a football party, or just for your family at home. It's easy on the wallet because it has few ingredients and is great when served with garlic bread.

Ingredients

  • pound mostaccioli pasta
  • 1 (15.5 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • thawed 1 (8 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese
  • more to taste 12 frozen cooked meatballs
